About

Anime Prompt Studio is a free, browser-based tool designed to help artists and enthusiasts craft detailed, high-quality prompts for AI image generation.

Built with a focus on anime and illustration styles, it gives you full control over character appearance, outfit, mood, scene, lighting, camera, and more — all in one place.

No account required. No data collected. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Tips & Tricks
01
Start with Style

Always pick an Art Style or Anime Studio first — it's the single most impactful tag for the final look.

02
Use the Strength Meter

Watch the Prompt Strength score. Conflicts (red) hurt quality the most — fix those before adding more tags.

03
Lighting is Everything

Add at least one lighting type. Rim Light + one ambient source gives dramatic, professional-looking results.

04
Tag Weight Slider

Click any tag in the prompt box to open the weight slider. Boost key elements like style or lighting above 1.3 for more impact.

05
Quality Tags Matter

Always include 3–4 quality tags: Masterpiece, Best Quality, Ultra Detailed. These are expected by most anime models.

06
Less is More

3–4 lighting types or 5+ effects will confuse the model. Keep each category focused — the Overload warning will alert you.

07
Save Your Favourites

Found a great combination? Hit the ♡ button to save it. You can load it any time from the Favourites menu.

08
Use Extra Tags

The Extra Positive Tags field is perfect for model-specific tokens or very niche details not covered by the builder.

09
Character Library v2

Click the ★ star on any character card to save it to your library. Open the library anytime from the gender select screen. You can upload a personal photo for each saved character by clicking the photo box.

10
Blueprint Lock v2

Long-press (hold 0.5s) or double-click any cell in the Character Blueprint to lock it. Locked cells turn green and show a 🔒 icon. Their values are preserved when you hit Random — perfect for keeping a style or scene while randomizing the character.

11
Dual Characters v2

Each character slot has fully independent settings. Switch between Character 1 and 2 using the tabs — each gets their own hair, outfit, mood, and tools. The Smart Randomizer gives both characters different appearances automatically.
